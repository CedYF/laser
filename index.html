Quick Procedure, Lasting Benefits

The latest procedures are so advanced that your vision can be corrected in around twenty minutes.

More than 95% of people happy with laser eye surgery results, according the the Royal College of Ophthalmologists.1 Laser eye can cure a number of conditions including short-sightedness, long-sightedness and astigmatism.

Clinics also offer payment plans, meaning you can pay as little as £19 per month per eye for treatment at the UK’s top clinics - far cheaper than contact lenses in the long run.

The operation uses a cool beam of light to reshape the transparent layer at the front of the eye. Vision is corrected within one day and most people can return to work within two. Recovery is quick and no bandages or stitches are usually required, giving you instant results.

And, with no worries about forgetting your glasses or spare contacts, you'll win back your sense of spontaneity as well.
